1. Sponsor

This contest is sponsored by Kratz Homes and Developments, Regina, Saskatchewan, Canada ("Sponsor"). This promotion is in no way sponsored, endorsed, administered by, or associated with Instagram or Facebook.

2. Contest Period

The contest begins on June 23, 2026 and all entries must be received by 11:59 PM Central Standard Time (CST) on July 3, 2026 ("Entry Deadline"). Entries received after this time will not be eligible.

3. Eligibility

Open to Canadian residents who are 18 years of age or older at the time of entry and who are planning to build or are considering building a residential home in Saskatchewan. Employees of Kratz Homes and Developments and their immediate family members are not eligible. Void where prohibited by law.

4. How to Enter

To enter, you must complete all four of the following steps during the Contest Period:

  • Follow @kratzhomesregina on Instagram
  • Like the official giveaway post on the @kratzhomesregina Instagram feed
  • Comment on the giveaway post tagging your partner and stating which neighbourhood in Saskatchewan you would build your dream home
  • Complete and submit the official entry form at kratzhomes.ca/giveaway

Bonus Entry: Reshare the official giveaway post to your Instagram Story and tag @kratzhomesregina to receive one (1) additional entry. Limit one bonus entry per person.

Limit: One (1) entry per person. Multiple entries will result in disqualification.

5. Prize

One (1) prize consisting of two (2) Platinum Weekend Passes to Country Thunder Saskatchewan 2026, taking place July 10–12, 2026 in Craven, Saskatchewan. Approximate retail value: $1,200 CAD.

The prize must be accepted as awarded and is non-transferable. No cash substitute. Sponsor is not responsible for any costs associated with attending the event, including transportation, accommodation, food, or incidentals.

6. Draw and Winner Selection

One (1) winner will be selected by random draw on July 4, 2026 from all eligible entries received by the Entry Deadline. The draw will be conducted by Kratz Homes and Developments at their Regina, Saskatchewan offices.

The selected winner will be contacted by direct message on Instagram and by email within 48 hours of the draw. The winner must respond within 72 hours of notification or an alternate winner will be selected.
