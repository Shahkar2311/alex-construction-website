Our Story

Building Regina since before most builders existed.

Kratz Homes has been part of Regina for 75 years — building through every season this city has had. Three generations later, we're still family-run, still local, and still building homes we'd be proud to live in ourselves.

This isn't a franchise. There's no head office making decisions for us. When you call Kratz, you talk to the people who will actually build your home.

The Process

How we build together.

Building a custom home should feel collaborative, not overwhelming. Here's how we make it work.

1

Discovery Call

Free, no-obligation conversation. We listen to your vision, ask the right questions, and give you honest answers about what's realistic.

2

Design & Planning

We work through lot selection, floor plans, finishes, and a real budget — so you know exactly what you're getting before a single shovel goes in the ground.

3

Your Build

We handle permits, scheduling, suppliers, and every trade. You get regular updates and site access anytime — this is your home, and you're always welcome.

4

Move-In Day

A complete walkthrough, everything working, everything finished. We hand over the keys and stay reachable if you ever have questions down the road.
